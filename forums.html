Forums — coming soon

A community space for IRLid users, venue operators, and curious technologists is on the roadmap. Until it ships, the best places to discuss the protocol and share questions are:

For now

  • Email support@irlid.co.uk for questions, bug reports, or feature ideas.
  • See the About page for the protocol design and rationale.
  • See Features for a current capabilities overview.

What's planned

The forums will give:

  • Public threads on protocol design, deployment patterns, and trust-network ideas.
  • A space for venue operators to share check-in flows and lessons learned.
  • Member-tier discussions for ongoing supporters of the project.

If you'd like to be notified when forums launch, drop a one-line email to support@irlid.co.uk with the subject line "forums".
